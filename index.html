Bio

I am a machine learning researcher and computational astrophysicist with a passion for applying state-of-the-art tech to solve pressing, meaningful challenges. I received my PhD in 2024 from the Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics at the University of California, Santa Cruz working with Enrico Ramirez-Ruiz. My thesis research focused on high-energy astrophysics and fluid dynamics in interacting stellar binaries. I completed a BS Physics at the University of Colorado Denver in 2017 after earning a BA Theatre from Meredith College and touring nationally as a musician and performing artist (and traveling the world) for nearly a decade.

My non-research interests include bridging science and society, equitable and inclusive STEM, and science advocacy and policy. Personal interests include native and edible gardening, contemporary/aerial dance and performing arts, cultivating mindfulness, and spending quality time with my family.
